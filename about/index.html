What is LCSC?

The Latino/a Computer Science Club became an official RSO in the Spring of 2007. The club promotes students who share an interest in Computer Science and provides a welcoming environment for them to get to know each other.

Activites That We Host

LCSC hosts a variety of activities throughout the year, such as bowling, game nights, and live showings of soccer games on the big screens of the Siebel Center. Our biggest event of the year is the Fiesta, which happens during the Spring semester. We usually have over 100 people who come out to eat Mexican food with us, wear beads, and even dance salsa!

Want to Get Involved?

LCSC is relatively small so there is a lot of opportunity for new members to have a big impact on the future of the club. Either come to one of our events, or email one of our Co-Presidents.
