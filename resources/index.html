Scholarships

Hispanic Scholarship Fund

The Hispanic Scholarship Fund awards merit based scholarships to students of hispanic heritage with an emphasis on STEM majors.

SHPE Scholarships

The Society of Hispanic Professional Engineers offer many scholarships depending on application.

Box Diversity Scholarship

Scholarships available to students who are studying in a STEAM (science, technology, engineering, art, math) or related major and identify with an underepresented group such as Latinos.

Generation Google Scholarship

Scholarship from google available to students pursuing a degree in computer science, computer engineering, or a closely related field, that are from an underrepresented group in computer science such as Latinos.

Microsoft Scholarships

Multiple scholarships available for students pursuing a degree in Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related STEM field.
