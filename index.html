Latinx in Computer Science @ University of Illinois

The Latinx Computer Science Club's main objective is to create a special community within the Computer Science department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in order to retain, support and foster a close relationship among the underrepresented students.

Mission Statment

We are Latino/as in Computer Science, a recognized student organization under the Computer Science department at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. We are a non-profit, educational organization aimed at supporting underrepresented students who are interested in pursuing Computer Science as a career, offering them educational and networking opportunities to help them achieve their goals in the Computer Science field. Recognizing the need for inclusion of Latinos in Computer Science at Illinois, we aid the University by retaining these underrepresented students.
